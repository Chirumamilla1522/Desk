        Interview prep

        STAR story bank — a small set of deep behavioral stories (Situation, Task, Action, Result, plus Reflection) that you reuse and adapt for “Tell me about a time…” instead of memorizing dozens of one-off answers. Offer evaluations often suggest new stories; you merge and polish them here.

        Company prep — one notebook per employer or loop: what they need, why you fit, questions you will ask them, and notes after each round. Live practice below can combine that notebook with your STAR bank when you pick a ledger row.

        Live interview practice

        Split screen like a real loop: their line on the left — behavioral prompts plus text from your STAR story bank; when you start with a ledger row, it also uses questions from that role’s company prep. Your side is a rough spoken transcript — timed rounds so you do not ramble.

        or

        Behavioral practice using only your STAR story bank — no company notebook until you start with a role above.
